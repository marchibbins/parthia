PARTHIA was launched to deliver a creative business but with a more sustainable business culture. Spending more time thinking and producing and less on overheads and bureaucracy. Giving our clients a better return on investment, and a more dynamic, conducive and transparent company to work with.

We’ll always invest in the project, not blow it on fine dining and swanky hotels. PARTHIA is upfront, honest, hardworking and passionate. We deliver what we promise, for when we promised it and for what it cost.

PARTHIA sets out to conceive and produce standout content that drives both audience engagement and behavior. We’re in the business of understanding and cherishing stories, because at PARTHIA we believe storytelling is the key to creating compelling brand identity.

We make sure the top talent is working on your project, in a similar vein to how film studios work, accessing global talent, bringing it all together to develop great ideas and crafting them into powerful films that populate culture.
