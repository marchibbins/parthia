PARTHIA’S production wing sees projects cross-pollinate between film, print, advertising, press, content and design. Whether it's a case study, book or website design, b2c, b2b, an online commercial or a fully integrated brand campaign, PARTHIA approaches all projects with a shared commitment and passion for innovation, collaboration and great storytelling.

In addition to the small roster of film directors we work with, we have an extensive team of talented people with rich portfolios covering advertising, music videos, motion graphics, photography, fashion, long and short form documentary and animation.

We’re a lean, nimble and multi-disciplinary company with strong working relationships across the creative industries, giving us a global reach and support network at any given time.
